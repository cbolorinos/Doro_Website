NEW CONSTRUCTION

We are a full-service Sarasota construction and real estate services firm. So we can serve your needs in any stage of the process of Florida property development. We have over 25 years of experience, both with commercial and residential property. We can build you a custom home with acute attention to detail and our personal service for the discriminating home buyer. Our expertise includes green homes because we understand the importance of sustainability. We build healthy, efficient, and comfortable new homes, saving our customers thousands of dollars on their energy bills, and saving our environment. Whatever your needs, we deliver on time, on budget, to the exact standards you expect. Come talk to us about your personalized new dream home!

REMODELING

Need your home expanded, weatherized, modernized? All three? The Vision Group can do all that and more. We have extensive experience building and remodeling in the Sarasota area, with long-standing relationships in the Sarasota community, cultivated over many years based on trust and attention to detail. We are your single source for Sarasota home repair and remodeling; our services are offered all in-house to ensure the highest quality standards and service.
