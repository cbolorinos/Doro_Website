Communities We've Served

Arlington Park
Established in 1979, Arlington Park is owned by the city of Sarasota and operated by Sarasota County Parks and Recreation. With the variety of recreational as well as aquatic activities are offered here, many refer to it as their "home away from home." The area around this park is currently exploding with new homes and remodels. Values are soaring and it is by far the most popular area for people who want to be proximal to downtown and still maintain a budget. Amenities include: 50-meter lap pool, 25-yard instructional pool, wading pool, indoor & outdoor basketball courts, outdoor tennis courts with lights, outdoor racquetball court with lights, ½-mile paved walking trail, weight room and picnic area, and more!
Cherokee Park and Harbor Acres
HARBOR ACRES: Developed from the dredging of Sarasota Bay, Harbor Acres was formed with two peninsulas that extend into the bay, creating a boater’s paradise with deep water access to Sarasota Bay and the Gulf of Mexico. This prestigious community overlooks Bird Key, which ironically was also created from dredging projects by some of the great city planners of the day. Home Sites in Harbor Acres are typically 1/3 of an acre and larger.
CHEROKEE PARK: A choice bayside location is the iconic Cherokee Park, another of the city’s oldest and most beautiful neighborhoods. In its earliest days the neighborhood was considered far from downtown Sarasota, but is now prized as an oasis amid a bustling city. Although it dates back to the ‘20s, most of the homes were built in the boom after World War II. Styles vary, but most reflect traditional or Spanish styles and nearly every home has been impressively remodeled to preserve the special character of the lovely neighborhood. Today, these 1/2 acre home sites are perfect for the discerning homebuyer seeking space and privacy in an urban setting.

About Mike

Mike Padgett is a native of Sarasota and has been involved in all facets of real estate for over 25 years. Fresh out of college in 1991, Mike joined his father and uncle in their residential home building endeavor, Padgett Homes. Through this, he learned the Sarasota new home builder business from “slab to sales.” Michael spent nearly 10 years absorbing information, processes, and most importantly the work ethic to help make every client’s biggest Sarasota Florida property purchase come to fruition. The Vision Group is a continuation of that ethic. Furthermore, in 2009, Michael earned his real estate license and joined forces with Coffey & Co., one of the most up and coming purveyors of fine homes in Sarasota.
The most important thing when choosing a Sarasota Florida realtor is to find someone you can trust. Mike has a history of success and integrity in Sarasota real estate that you can truly depend on. He is an experienced negotiator with expert knowledge of the local Sarasota Florida property market. About Cary

A graduate of Florida State University in Marketing, Cary Vorbeck brings over 20 years of commercial real estate experience to the Vision Team. Cary has resided and worked all over the state of Florida in such places as Sarasota, Bradenton, Tallahassee, Daytona Beach, Tarpon Springs, and Arcadia. His experience dealing with different areas of florida brings a unique perspective to the Vision Group. Throughout his long career in real estate, Cary has acquired extensive knowledge on topics such as Sarasota commercial property assessment, 1031 tax deferred exchanges, project development, and commercial construction. He strives to help clients feel comfortable and fully understand all of the facets of the business. Cary is currently managing a private portfolio of commercial buildings throughout the state of Florida that is a mix of warehouse, retail and office space, in addition to multi-family rental communities. Along with managing a portfolio of properties throughout the state of florida, he has international experience in contract negotiations specializing in Central America. As an active member of the Sarasota community, Cary is a Free Mason, member of the Sarasota 100 Club (a Police benevolent Association), and member of the Sarasota Board of Realtors.
