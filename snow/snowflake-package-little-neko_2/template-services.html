Commercial Services

Looking to buy or sell a Sarasota commercial property? Perhaps you are looking to take advantage of a tax deferred 1031 Exchange? Or are you looking for efficiency and expertise related to any other commercial real estate services?

Here at the Vision Group, we can gladly assist you with these services and more. We have a dedicated and knowledgeable commercial real estate team managed by Cary Vorbeck, who has over 20 years of commercial real estate experience. A graduate of Florida State University in marketing, Cary brings extensive knowledge on Sarasota commercial property evaluation, 1031 tax deferred exchanges, project development and commercial construction. He is currently managing a private portfolio of commercial buildings throughout the state of Florida that is a mixture of warehouse, retail, office space, and mobile home park communities. Come to Cary at the Vision Group for any of your commercial real estate needs!
